Privacy Policy for AwarenessHub

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to AwarenessHub ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and password.
  • Profile Information: You may provide additional information such as your name and profile picture.
  • Learning Progress: We collect information about your progress through courses, quiz results, and challenge completions.
  • Survey Responses: When you provide feedback through surveys, we collect your responses.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including features used, time spent, and navigation patterns.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Log Data: Our servers automatically record information when you use the app, including IP address, access times, and crash reports.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: To create and manage your account, deliver educational content, and track your learning progress.
  • Personalization: To customize your learning experience and recommend relevant content.
  • Leaderboards: To display your ranking and achievements in comparison with other users (using anonymized usernames).
  • Communication: To send you important updates, notifications about new content, and respond to your inquiries.
  • Improve Services: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and enhance app functionality.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, and security threats.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase as our backend service provider, which stores data securely in cloud infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/SSL
  • Secure password storage using bcrypt hashing
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Supabase and other trusted service providers who assist in operating our app.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulations.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of AwarenessHub, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified).

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access and review your account information at any time through the app.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information in your profile settings.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
  • Export: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of non-essential communications through app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Backend database and authentication service. See their privacy policy at https://supabase.com/privacy
  • Vercel: Web hosting service. See their privacy policy at https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your session and keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze app usage and performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect app functionality.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using our app, you consent to such transfers.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your information within 90 days, except where retention is required by law.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Your Consent

By using AwarenessHub, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us:

  • Email: support@awarenesshub.app
  • GitHub: AwarenessHub Repository
